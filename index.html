CNC Milling (3 & 5 Axis)

Technical Specs

Doosan DNM 5700 & Mori Seiki

High-speed machining on modern vertical centers. Ideal for complex geometries, tools, and housings from various materials such as steel, aluminum, stainless steel, and brass.

  • Travels (X / Y / Z): 1050 / 570 / 510 mm

Technical Specs
