Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

1. General Information

Global CNC (Global CNC d.o.o. Teslić) takes the protection of your personal data very seriously[cite: 2]. This policy explains how we collect and process your information when you use our website.

2. Data Collection via RFQ / Contact Form

When you use our technical quote form, we collect your name, company name, email address, quantities, and technical files (drawings/3D models)[cite: 2, 3]. This data is used solely to evaluate manufacturability and calculate your quote, and is never shared without your explicit consent[cite: 3].

3. Server Log Files

Our hosting provider automatically collects and stores information in "server log files," which your browser automatically transmits to us. These include IP address, browser type, and time of request. This data is not merged with other data sources.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about your stored personal data at any time, free of charge. You also have the right to request that it be corrected, blocked, or deleted.

5. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at: info@globalcnc.co[cite: 2]
